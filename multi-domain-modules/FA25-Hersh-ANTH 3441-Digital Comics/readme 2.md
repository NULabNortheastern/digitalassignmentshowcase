<h2>Summary of Module</h2>
This module introduces students to effective digital comic creation. We will explore a range of methods to help students identify the approach that best aligns with their creative goals and technical comfort. 

<h2>Learning Goals</h2>

* Understand best practices for digital comic creation.
 Explore three major approaches:
* Hand-drawing and scanning, followed by digital clean-up or modification.
* Drawing on tablets with styluses, using digital art software.
* Using Pixton, an accessible platform for building comics quickly with templates.

<h2>Learning Objectives</h2>
Learn how to choose the most appropriate method based on your artistic goals, timeline, and skill level.
