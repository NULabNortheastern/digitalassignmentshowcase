<h2>Summary of Module</h2>

These modules will introduce students to effective digital comic creation, the basics of audio recording and editing podcasts, and the fundamentals of video production and editing. 

<h2>Learning Goals</h2>

<h3>Digital Comics with Pixton</h3>

* Understand best practices for digital comic creation.
* Explore three major approaches:
* Hand-drawing and scanning, followed by digital clean-up or modification.
* Drawing on tablets with styluses, using digital art software.
* Using Pixton, an accessible platform for building comics quickly with templates.

<h3>Audio Recording with Audacity</h3>

* Understand components for a compelling podcast.
* Outline the considerations and preparation needed for building a podcast.
* Understand how to use record audio with Audacity.

<h3>Audio Editing with Audacity</h3>

* Understand how to edit a podcast.
* Practice using Audacity to add, clip, fade in and out, and split audio tracks.
* Learn how to save, export, and provide metadata for your project.

<h3>Intro to Video Production</h3>

* Understand the steps in video production
* Learn how to avoid common pitfalls in video production

<h3>Video Editing with Vimeo</h3>

* Learn how to use Vimeo for basic video editing tasks 
* Gain awareness of other free video editing software
