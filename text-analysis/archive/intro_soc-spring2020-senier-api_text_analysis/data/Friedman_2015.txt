Opioid painkillers are deadly, overprescribed and often ineffective.THERE has been an alarming and steady increase in the mortality rate of middle-aged white Americans since 1999, according to a study published last week. This increase — half a percent annually — contrasts starkly with decreasing death rates in all other age and ethnic groups and with middle-aged people in other developed countries.Opioid painkillers are deadly, overprescribed and often ineffective.
