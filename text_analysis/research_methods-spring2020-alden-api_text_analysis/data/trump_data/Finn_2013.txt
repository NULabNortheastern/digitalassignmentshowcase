A floor-through unit at the Trump Palace with six terraces and views of Central Park sold for $15 million.A floor-through combination unit at the Trump Palace with six terraces and gravity-defying vistas of the cityscape and Central Park sold for $15 million and was the most expensive sale of the week, according to city records. The four-bedroom, three-bath residence, No. 47A/B, had an initial asking price of $16.95 million and has monthly carrying charges of $14,425.A floor-through unit at the Trump Palace with six terraces and views of Central Park sold for $15 million.
