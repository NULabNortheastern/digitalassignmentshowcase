It lost, to “How to Survive a Plague,” but may still be in the running for the Oscars; the documentary shortlist is expected to be announced soon. But Ms. Abramovic, who conquered the art world – the documentary chronicles her 2010 retrospective at the Museum of Modern Art – is learning that finding her footing in the film world is a different beast.
