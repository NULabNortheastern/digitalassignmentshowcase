His ego may be shatterproof, but the glassware isn’t.Donald Trump isn’t afraid to put his name on things — at last count, he had more than a dozen licensees making products bearing his name, including steaks and eyewear. But unlike other high-profile types, who lend their names to things they never actually use, Mr. Trump seems to delight in surrounding himself with Trump products.
