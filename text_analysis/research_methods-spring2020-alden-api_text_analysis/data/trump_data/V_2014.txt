Vera Coking’s Atlantic City home — once coveted by Donald Trump, who had offered her seven figures — will be up for auction with a reserve price of $199,000.ATLANTIC CITY — A stand of blossoming lilac trees and a fresh coat of white paint hardly hide the decrepitude of the three-story boardinghouse half a block from the boardwalk. Ripped screens cover the windows that have not been smashed or boarded up. In the kitchen, refrigerators stand open and empty in a row; dirty plates fill the sink. Some guest rooms look untouched, beds made, while bags of trash and piles of suitcases litter others. What appears to be a crack pipe sits on a dresser.Vera Coking’s Atlantic City home — once coveted by Donald Trump, who had offered her seven figures — will be up for auction with a reserve price of $199,000.
