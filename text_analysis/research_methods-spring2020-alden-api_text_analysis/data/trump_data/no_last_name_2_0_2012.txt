After lots of storm damage, a beach gets a break when Donald Trump’s project is canceled.New York State parks officials have a huge job ahead of them in repairing Jones Beach State Park, which was dilapidated before Hurricane Sandy and devastated after, its boardwalk crumpled and its buildings ravaged. The park’s central mall, designed in Robert Moses’ day to evoke an ocean liner, today looks like a ship’s deck after a bad encounter with a very large, cold, floating object somewhere in the North Atlantic.
